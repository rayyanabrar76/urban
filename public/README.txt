Drop your transparent brand logo here as:

    logo.png

It will automatically appear in:
  - the header + footer badge (~40px)
  - the hero showcase tile (large, ~240px)

Until logo.png exists, both spots fall back gracefully
(header/footer show "US", the hero shows "PSG / Back to Back").

Anything you put in this /public folder is served from the site root,
e.g.  public/logo.png  ->  http://localhost:3000/logo.png

You can delete this README once your logo is in place.
